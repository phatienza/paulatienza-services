// My Approach

Anxiety is not
a character flaw.
It's a problem to solve.

Most people with anxiety have spent years trying to "manage" it — deep breaths, avoiding triggers, white-knuckling through panic. That works, until it doesn't.

I work differently. Using evidence-based methods from cognitive-behavioral therapy and Acceptance and Commitment Therapy, we don't just cope with anxiety — we change your relationship to it entirely. The goal isn't to feel less. It's to do more.

01

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy

Identifying and restructuring the thought patterns driving your anxiety cycle.

02

Acceptance & Commitment

Stop fighting your mind. Start acting on your values regardless of how you feel.

03

Exposure Response Prevention

Systematic, supported exposure that actually reduces anxiety over time.

04

Performance Psychology

For executives and high-achievers: optimize under pressure, not despite it.
