About Dr. Novak

Therapy that honors
the whole relationship

I became a couples therapist because I believe relationships are worth the hard work — but that work should feel purposeful, not punishing.

My practice is warm, structured, and rooted in evidence. I draw primarily from the Gottman Method and Emotionally Focused Therapy — two of the most rigorously researched approaches for couples — while always adapting to what you actually need in the room.

I work with couples navigating communication breakdowns, infidelity recovery, life transitions, premarital preparation, and the quiet drift that happens when life gets loud.
